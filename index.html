FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How much does aluminium work cost?

Cost varies based on material quality, design, and size. We provide detailed quotations after site visit and measurements. Contact us for a free quote.

Do you provide free site visits?

Yes, we provide free site visits and consultations in Jodhpur and nearby areas.

Do you install toughened glass?

Yes, we specialize in toughened glass installation for windows, partitions, shower cubicles, and railings.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide warranty on materials and workmanship. Warranty period depends on the type of work and materials used.

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies by project size. Small projects take 1-3 days, while larger commercial projects may take 1-2 weeks.

Do you work outside Jodhpur?

Yes, we undertake projects in Jodhpur and nearby cities in Rajasthan.
